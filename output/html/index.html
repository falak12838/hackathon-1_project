What is Physical AI?

Estimated reading time: 1 minutes

# What is Physical AI? Physical AI refers to artificial intelligence systems that interact with the physical world through sensors, actuators, and robotic systems. Unlike traditional AI that operates primarily in digital spaces, Physical AI bridges the gap between computational intelligence and physical reality. ## Key Characteristics - Embodied intelligence - Real-world interaction - Sensorimotor integration - Adaptive behavior in physical environments

What are Humanoid Robots?

Estimated reading time: 1 minutes

# What are Humanoid Robots? Humanoid robots are robots designed with a human-like body structure. They typically have a head, torso, two arms, and two legs, though some may have variations. ## Key Components - **Actuators**: Motors that enable movement - **Sensors**: Cameras, touch sensors, and other devices for perception - **Control Systems**: Software and algorithms for movement and behavior - **Power Systems**: Batteries or other power sources
