# What are Humanoid Robots?

Humanoid robots are robots designed with a human-like body structure. They typically have a head, torso, two arms, and two legs, though some may have variations.

## Key Components

- **Actuators**: Motors that enable movement
- **Sensors**: Cameras, touch sensors, and other devices for perception
- **Control Systems**: Software and algorithms for movement and behavior
- **Power Systems**: Batteries or other power sources
