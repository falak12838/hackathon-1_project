# History and Evolution of Physical AI

The development of Physical AI has its roots in early cybernetics and robotics research. This chapter explores the historical development and key milestones in the field.

## Early Foundations

The field began with pioneers like Norbert Wiener and his work on cybernetics in the 1940s-50s. This was followed by early robotics research in the 1960s.
